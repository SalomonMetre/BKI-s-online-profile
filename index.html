Biography

Bienfait Kabuyaya Isamura

is a 1 st year PhD candidate in Theoretical Chemistry at the University of Manchester. He obtained his master of science degree in Chemistry with distinction from Rhodes University and was awarded the 2022 S2A3 medal for original research at master’s level. BKI has published nine research articles, among which five first-authored articles. His research interest includes the investigation of noncovalent interactions, method benchmarking and the development of new force fields.
